Arroz con Gandules (Rice and Pigeon Peas)

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 cloves of garlic
  • 1 chopped green pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pack of saz√≥n (coriander & annatto)
  • chopped cilantro
  • 1 can of Goya pigeon peas
  • 1 can of Goya tomato sauce
  • 2 cups of rice
  • olive oil
  • salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Rinse rice in water.
  2. With a large pot, heat oil and add tomato sauce. Simmer for two minutes.
  3. Stir in gandules, spices and 4 cups of water. Add salt until the broth is heavy and salty.
  4. Boil it rapidly and add the rice to the pot. Reduce heat to soft boil and let most of the liquid absorb the rice.
  5. Reduce heat to simmer and cook for 20 minutes. Test the rice after 20 minutes and adjust as needed.
  6. Enjoy your delicious arroz con gandules!
