Mofongo

Ingredients:

  • 3 unripe plantains
  • garlic
  • 10 oz skirt steak
  • kosher salt
  • olive oil
  • mortar and pestle

Steps:

  1. Pour 2 inches of olive oil in a deep fryer at 350F
  2. Peel and cut the plantains into 1-inch rounds/squares
  3. Put the cut plantains into the heated oil and wait until they look golden, tender and crisp
  4. Fill the mortar with the plantains and garlic and start mashing them
  5. Cook the skirt steak to your liking
  6. Smooth the mashed plantains into a ball and add the steak on top of it
  7. Enjoy your delcious mofongo!
