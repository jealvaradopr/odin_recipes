Ingredient:

  • 8lb pork shoulder
  • 12 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of dried oregano
  • pack of saz√≥n (coriander & annatto)
  • 2 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 8 teaspoons of salt

Steps:

  1. Season all the ingredients together prior to roasting the meat.
  2. Preheat oven at 325 degrees.
  3. Place pork in roasting pan with foil.
  4. Bake for 5-6 hours at 325 degrees.
  5. Remove foil and bake at 375 for an hour.
  6. Once the pork skin is golden and crispy, remove from the oven.
  7. Enjoy your delicious pork!
